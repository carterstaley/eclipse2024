Best Locations for Viewing in the U.S.

People travel around the world to see a total eclipse of the sun. Make it a trip and visit a location on the path of totality. Here's a few of the best places in the U.S. to view totality in 2024.


San Antonio, TX

Be one of the first people in the U.S. to see the eclipse at 1:32 PM CT. In San Antonio, totality will last 2 minutes and 11 seconds.

Dallas, TX

In Dallas, the total eclipse starts at 1:40 PM CT and lasts a full 3 minutes and 50 seconds.

Little Rock, AR

Little Rock is one of the best places in Arkansas to see the eclipse. Totality will start at 1:51 PM CT and last 2 minutes and 31 seconds.

Cape Girardeau, MO

Head to the banks of Big Muddy to watch the total eclipse at 1:58 PM CT for a whole 4 minutes and 6 seconds!

Carbondale, IL

Carbondale was prime viewing for the 2017 eclipse and the upcomming 2024 eclipse! Totality starts at 1:59 PM CT and lasts 4 minutes and 8 seconds.

Indianpolis, IN

In Indianapolis, the total eclipse begins at 3:05 PM CT and lasts 3 minutes and 49 seconds.
